Harvey Dale and the Cements

Harvey Dale

Harvey Dale was born in Austin, Texas. He has been playing guitar since he was concieved. He has a dog, two cats, three birds and a wife. His favorite color is hot pink and zebra stripes.

Harvey Pale

Harvey Pale was born in Pheonix, Arizona. He refused to go out in the sun as a child, so his friends started calling him Harvey Pale. His brain got fried one time when he was water-skiing becasue he forgot to put a hat on his bald head. He can't remember his real name and lives with his momma.

Jennifer Lawrence

After losing the love of her life in the 995th Hunger Games, Jennifer Lawrence came to Austin, Texas looking for a tall glass of...milk. She walked into Emo's, crying and looking like a hot mess, where she ran into Harvey Dale and Harvey Pale. The rest is history.
